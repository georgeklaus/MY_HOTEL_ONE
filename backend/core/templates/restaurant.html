An Experience for the Senses
The Restaurant

Savor an exquisite culinary journey at our signature restaurant, where world-class flavors meet impeccable ambiance. Led by Chef Michael Martin, our dining experience celebrates a fusion of Asian and European influences, curated with the finest ingredients.

The restaurant’s open-concept studio kitchens allow you to witness the artistry behind each dish, from classic favorites to innovative gourmet creations. Wine and cheese connoisseurs will find delight in our Wine & Cheese Cellar, featuring an extensive selection of the world’s finest vintages, all housed in stunning five-meter-high glass walls.

  • Lunch: 12.00 noon – 2.00 pm (daily)
